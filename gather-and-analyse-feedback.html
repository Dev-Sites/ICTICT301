Quantitative Vs Qualitative 

Quantitative 

- Collects data in the form of statistical numbers
- Analyses data for actual trends, insights and  identify patterns over a period of time
- Conducts research into:
How much? How Many? How Often? What Size? 


Qualitative

- Asks questions to gauge information 
- Seeks opinions, ideas and motivations
- Explores perceptions and reasons

Final Steps 

Finalising and presenting your user document

step one 

Seek Target Audience Feedback 

Go directly to your niche market where your target audience are and seek their opinion and experiences with your product or service; focusing in particular on this specific pitchdeck document you have created

step two 

Gather and Analyse 

Collate all the qualitative and quantitative data you have gathered. Align all similarities and isolate the most common area for immediate improvement on your pitchdeck

step three

Adapt, Submit and Present

Validate all the improvements which can be made and adapt your original pitchdeck document to represent these improvements. Proofread final document, submit for approval from your client or business lead and then confidently present to your Mastermind group, your first Pitchdeck presentation.
